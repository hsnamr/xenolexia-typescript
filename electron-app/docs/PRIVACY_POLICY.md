# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Introduction

Xenolexia ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Books and Documents:** EPUB files you import into the app
- **Vocabulary Data:** Words you save, including context sentences
- **Reading Progress:** Your position in books and reading statistics
- **Preferences:** App settings like language selection, theme, and font choices

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, address)
- Account credentials (no account required)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking
- Usage analytics or behavioral data

## How Your Data Is Stored

**All data is stored locally on your device.** We do not have servers that store your personal information.

- Books are stored in the app's private storage
- Vocabulary and progress are stored in a local SQLite database
- Preferences are stored in device secure storage

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties.**

The app may connect to the internet only for:
- Downloading free ebooks from public sources (optional)
- Translation API requests (optional, for word lookups)

These connections do not transmit any personal data.

## Third-Party Services

### Translation APIs

When you tap on words, the app may use free translation APIs:
- LibreTranslate (open source)
- MyMemory Translation API
- Lingva Translate

These services receive only the individual words being translated, not your personal information or reading data.

### Book Sources

When browsing free books, the app connects to:
- Project Gutenberg (gutenberg.org)
- Standard Ebooks (standardebooks.org)
- Open Library (openlibrary.org)

These connections follow each service's privacy policies.

## Data Security

Your data is protected by:
- Device-level encryption (if enabled on your device)
- App sandboxing (iOS and Android security models)
- No network transmission of personal data

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

- **Access:** View all your data within the app
- **Export:** Export vocabulary to CSV, Anki, or JSON
- **Delete:** Clear all data from Settings ‚Üí Data Management
- **Portability:** Your exported data can be used in other apps

## Children's Privacy

Xenolexia does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app contains no features that would attract children specifically, and no account creation is required.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Email:** privacy@xenolexia.app
- **Website:** https://xenolexia.app/privacy
